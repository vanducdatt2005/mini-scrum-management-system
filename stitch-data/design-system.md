# Design System Specification: Tactile Precision

## 1. Overview & Creative North Star
This design system is built upon the North Star of **"Tactile Precision."** In a world of cluttered, line-heavy management tools, we aim for a high-end editorial experience that feels as much like a premium architectural magazine as it does a productivity suite. 

We move beyond the "standard SaaS" look by replacing rigid grids and 1px borders with **Tonal Volumes**. The interface should feel like layered sheets of fine vellum and frosted glass. We embrace intentional asymmetry—using large typography scales and generous white space—to guide the eye, ensuring that a "Mini Scrum" workflow feels effortless and expensive, rather than mechanical.
